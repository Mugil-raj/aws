History of ChatGPT

2018

OpenAI releases GPT-2, the precursor to ChatGPT, which gains attention for its advanced natural language processing capabilities.

2019

OpenAI introduces GPT-2.5, an intermediate version of the model, with some improvements over the original.

2020

OpenAI releases ChatGPT, a specialized version of GPT-2 fine-tuned for conversational interactions.

2021

ChatGPT becomes widely adopted across various platforms and applications, demonstrating its effectiveness in chatbots and virtual assistants.

2022

OpenAI continues to improve ChatGPT with updates and refinements, enhancing its understanding and response capabilities.
